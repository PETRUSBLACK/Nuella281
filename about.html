All our recipes are professionally developed, rigorously tested, and designed to work in real home kitchens so they’ll taste just as delicious at your table as they do at ours. We also help ease dinnertime anxiety with simple yet effective cooking tips, recommendations for grocery and kitchen products (the same ones that we use ourselves), and fresh ideas to inspire your cooking.

Simply Recipes’ team of editors, recipe developers and testers, writers, and photographers are the primary cooks and grocery shoppers for our families, just like our readers. That means we understand your needs—because they’re our needs too. Whether it’s our series of one-ingredient upgrades passed down through generations, smart meal prep tips for the next holiday, or a simple Coca-Cola peanut snack that reminds us of childhood summers, our recipes and stories reflect the many kitchens, cultures, and traditions of our families.
